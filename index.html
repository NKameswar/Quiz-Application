Quiz Rules

Here are the rules for the quiz:

1.You are presented with a set of five questions that require your responses.

2.Each question offers four options, and you must select a single correct answer from among them.

3.For each correct answer, you will earn one point, and there is no penalty for incorrect responses.

4.You have a maximum time limit of 20 seconds to provide your answer to each question.

5.If you are unable to answer within the allotted time, the system will automatically advance you to the next question.
